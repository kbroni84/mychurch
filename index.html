PERSONAL MINISTRY

We love to reach out to souls!

Personal Ministry is all about encouraging members to present Jesus Christ to all peoples. This work calls for an integrated approach where the focus is not only centred on preachers only but all other activities that go a long way to reach out to people. Thus the use of multiple talents is greatly encouraged and most welcome as we work together for Jesus Christ. We reach out to people just where they are instead of waiting for them to come to church.
